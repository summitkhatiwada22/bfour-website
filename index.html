Crackamite

Crackamite is a high-performance, non-explosive demolition agent designed for safe and efficient rock breaking, concrete cutting, and controlled demolition without the risks of blasting. B Four Engineering & Construction has successfully supplied Crackamite for numerous hydropower and road projects across Nepal, helping engineers complete challenging excavation works safely, quietly, and cost-effectively.

Pneumatic Rock Drills

Pneumatic Rock Drills are powerful and reliable machines engineered for efficient drilling in tough rock surfaces. Designed for durability and high performance in demanding environments, they are ideal for mining, tunneling, and construction projects. B Four Engineering & Construction has successfully supplied HAVA Rock Drills for numerous hydropower and infrastructure projects across Nepal, delivering dependable solutions that enhance productivity and reduce downtime.
